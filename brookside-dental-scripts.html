Brookside Dental — Ad Campaign Scripts

Four video ad scripts for review

Four scripts for the next round of Brookside Dental video ads — one for new patients, two for implants, and one for dentures. Each is written to be read straight to camera. Take a look and let me know if they're good to go, and I'll load them into the teleprompter.

Scripts for Review

The Four Scripts

Script 1 — New Patient

Cleaning + free Sonicare for new patients
~30 seconds
Hook — No Shame
If it's been a while since you've been to the dentist, a couple years or longer, that's okay. I'm Dr. Christopherson, from Brookside Dental here in Cheyenne, Wyoming, and I'm not gonna give you a hard time about it. Nobody here is. We're just glad you came in.
Offer + Cost
Oh, and new patients get a Sonicare toothbrush, the electric one, just for coming in. There's a new-patient exam and cleaning, and we'll tell you the cost up front. No surprise bills.
First Visit
First visit, it's just a look and a cleaning. Nothing you don't agree to. We go at your pace, and we tell you what we're doing as we go. That's it.
CTA
Booking takes no time at all, you can do it right here. So if you've been putting it off, click Book Now. I'd like to meet you.

Script 2 — Implants

Direct — for anyone considering implants
~35 seconds
Hook — Name the Reason
If you've been thinking about implants, the thing holding you back is probably the cost. Or you're worried it's going to hurt. Or you just haven't gotten around to it yet. All of that's normal.
Cost
On cost, you'll know what it runs before you commit to anything, and we've got ways to break up the payments so it's not all at once. You're not going to get hit with a bill you didn't expect.
Pain
On the pain, we sedate you, we numb you, and most people tell me it was a lot easier than they thought.
Putting It Off
And if you've just been putting it off, the longer you wait, the more bone you lose. That doesn't mean we can't fix it. It just means the sooner we take a look, the more options you've got. No rush to decide anything, but it's worth coming in sooner than later.
CTA
You don't have to commit to anything to come in. Just come find out where you stand. Click Book Now.

Script 3 — Implants

Myth-busting — clears the reasons people talk themselves out of it
~40 seconds
Hook — The Five Myths
There are a lot of myths out there about full mouth dental implants. And if you've been putting it off because of something you heard, you're not the only one. So let me clear up the five biggest ones I hear all the time.
Myth 1 — They're Painful
First one is that implants hurt. That used to be more true, years back. But the way we do it now, we numb you up and we can sedate you so you're relaxed the whole time. Most folks tell me afterward it was a lot easier than they thought it'd be.
Myth 2 — They're Too Expensive
Second one is they cost too much. I won't pretend it's cheap. It's an investment. But think about dentures, you're replacing those every five to seven years, plus the glue and the refits. That keeps adding up. So over time, implants can actually end up costing you less.
Myth 3 — I'm Too Old
Third one, people figure they're too old for it. There's no age cutoff on this. What matters is your health, not the number. A lot of my implant patients are in their sixties and seventies, doing just fine.
Myth 4 — It Takes Too Long
Fourth, folks think it drags on forever. Not anymore. The process is a lot quicker than it used to be, and most people walk out with a new smile sooner than they were expecting.
Myth 5 — I'm Not Able to Do It
And the last one, somebody figures they already waited too long, or maybe another office told them no. Even then, there are things we can do, like bone grafting, to build it back up. Most people who come get a second look find out they can still do this after all.
CTA
You come in, I take a look, and you walk out knowing the real cost before you decide anything. No pressure either way. Click Book Now.

Script 4 — Dentures

For people who need first-time dentures, full or partial
~40 seconds
Hook — Name the Feeling
If somebody just told you that you need dentures, that's a hard thing to hear. A lot of people feel like they're getting old when they hear it. That's normal.
You May Keep Your Teeth
Dentures doesn't always mean losing all your teeth. If you've still got healthy ones, a lot of times we can keep those and just fill in the gaps. That's called a partial.
No Judgment
You don't have to be embarrassed about the shape your teeth are in. I've seen all of it. Nobody here is going to make a face or give you a lecture. And it's not too late, and needing this doesn't make you old or mean you did something wrong.
They Look Like You
The dentures we make now don't have to look fake or shift around when you talk. We fit them to you, so they look like your teeth and you can chew your food again.
Cost
On the cost, you'll know exactly what it's going to run you before you commit to anything, and we offer financing to help you take on the payments. I'm not going to push you toward implants you didn't come in for.
CTA
If you want to just come sit down and ask questions, that's fine. Click Book Now.
