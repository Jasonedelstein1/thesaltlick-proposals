What's Working in Creatives

1. Question-based hooks: The Crowns campaign's interrogative headline ("Will your crowns be covered?") achieved the highest hook rate at 29.93%, demonstrating that question-based hooks create curiosity and engagement.

2. Value-first messaging: The "FREE Sonicare" offer in the Free Toothbrush campaign proves that tangible incentives drive conversions with the lowest CPL and highest lead volume.

3. Visual elements: Emojis (👉, 👑) appear in successful headlines, suggesting visual breaks and emotional cues enhance engagement.

Creative Fatigue Indicators

All campaigns show ad fatigue: With frequencies between 6.52x and 14.25x, audiences have seen these ads too many times. Industry best practice suggests refreshing creative when frequency exceeds 4-5x to maintain performance.

Hook rate ranges: While all campaigns exceed the 10% benchmark, the Crowns campaign's 48.01% maximum hook rate (vs 11.11% minimum) shows creative quality variance within the same campaign, indicating some creatives are far superior to others.

Strategic Recommendations for Next Creative Batch

Messages to Double Down On

  • Free/Value-First Offers: The "FREE Sonicare" approach generated 95 leads at $58.49 CPL. Continue leading with tangible value propositions. Test variations like "Free Whitening Kit," "Free First Exam," or "Free Consultation."
  • Question-Based Hooks: The Crowns campaign's question format achieved the highest engagement. Develop more interrogative hooks such as: "Is your dental insurance covering what you need?" or "When was your last dental cleaning?"
  • Insurance/Coverage Angle: The "Will your crowns be covered?" message resonated despite low lead volume. This suggests financial concerns are a major consideration. Expand this angle across all services.
  • Target Female 25-44: This demographic has proven to be the highest ROI segment. Create creatives that speak directly to their concerns: family health, preventive care, cosmetic improvements.
  • Low-Barrier Entry Services: Cleanings with incentives outperformed high-value services significantly. Use cleanings as the gateway to upsell higher-value services.

New Creative Directions to Test

  • Urgency & Scarcity: Test headlines like "Last Chance: Free Sonicare Ending Soon" or "Only 10 Appointments Left This Month"
  • Problem-Solution Format: Address pain points directly: "Embarrassed by Your Smile? We Can Help" or "Dental Pain Keeping You Up? Same-Day Appointments Available"
  • Social Proof: Leverage testimonials and ratings: "Why 500+ Cheyenne Families Trust Brookside Dental" or "5-Star Rated Dental Care in Cheyenne"
  • Service-Specific Offers: Since implants/dentures had higher CPL, create targeted offers: "Implant Consultation + 3D Scan - Only $99" to lower barrier to entry
  • Before/After Visual Storytelling: The Crowns campaign's high hook rate suggests visual interest. Test before/after imagery with captions like "Real Patients, Real Results"
  • Seasonal/Timely Hooks: "New Year, New Smile - Book Your Cleaning Before February" or "Back to School Dental Checkups"

Creative Refresh Strategy

Immediate Actions (Next 30 Days)

1. Pause & Refresh Free Toothbrush Campaign: With 14.25x frequency, this campaign needs immediate creative rotation. Maintain the free offer but refresh visuals and copy angles.

2. Scale Crowns Creative Approach: The question-based format proved most engaging. Create 3-5 variations of question-based hooks for all services.

3. Increase Implants/Dentures Budget with Lower Barrier Offer: The high CPL suggests the direct "book exam" CTA is too aggressive. Test a softer approach with free consultation or educational content.

Conclusion & Next Steps

The current campaigns have established a strong foundation with 129 leads at $64.08 CPL, but are showing clear signs of creative fatigue. The data reveals that value-driven offers, question-based hooks, and targeting female audiences aged 25-44 are the winning formula.

The immediate priority is to refresh all creative assets while maintaining the proven messaging frameworks. By implementing the recommendations above, we can reduce CPL by 15-20% while increasing lead volume by 30%+ over the next 90 days.

Action Items
