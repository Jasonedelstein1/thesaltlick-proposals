Payments That Don't Eat Your Profits

Most platforms charge 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction. On a $10,000 invoice, that's $290 gone. With Conduit, your customers can pay via ACH bank transfer for just a $1 platform fee plus standard Stripe ACH processing (0.8% capped at $5). That same $10,000 invoice costs you $6 total, not $290. Customers pay directly from a link in their text message or email, no app download, no portal login.
