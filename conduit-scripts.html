Script 1

Have you ever thought about how much it's costing your business to collect a payment from a customer?

From this one project alone, you're losing $550 on fees and software costs, month after month. And that adds up. That could be a new truck wrap. That could be marketing that brings in new customers. That could be a pay raise for your employees. But instead of growing your business, that money is going to credit card companies and software companies.
Conduit invoices show your customer two prices, the bank transfer price and the card price, and they pick. A bank transfer costs you six bucks, even on a fifty-thousand-dollar invoice. And when your team grows, you're not buying another seat every single month — plans are priced by team size, at a fraction of what the big guys charge.
Migrating to Conduit isn't the project you think it is, either. Conduit connects to your old software and imports your customers, jobs, and invoices, while your old account just sits there untouched. You're just importing your stuff — you're not committing to anything.
Sign up for the free trial and migrate today. Next month, that $550 stays in your business.
