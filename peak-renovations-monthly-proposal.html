How the Retainer Works

Monthly payments remain consistent year-round, regardless of whether it's a production month or not. This makes budgeting simple and predictable. Payments are due on the 1st of each month via ACH or credit card. First month payment initiates the contract and schedules your first production session.

Bi-Monthly Package: $2,400/month for 12 months ($28,800 annually)
Quarterly Package: $1,983/month for 12 months ($23,800 annually)

Monthly payments are due on the 1st of each month. ACH/bank transfer is our standard payment method at the rates quoted above. Credit card payment is available with a 3% convenience fee to cover processing costs (Bi-Monthly: $2,472/month, Quarterly: $2,043/month).

Bi-Monthly Package: Production sessions in months 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11 (6 total annually)
Quarterly Package: Production sessions in months 1, 4, 7, 10 (4 total annually)

Each session is scheduled 2-3 weeks in advance to coordinate your availability and any job sites we want to feature. Sessions typically take 2-4 hours on-site and yield 3 complete video ads delivered within 7-10 days of filming.

Management is continuous and happens every month regardless of production schedule. I monitor all active campaigns daily, make strategic adjustments weekly, and provide monthly performance reports. As new video ads are delivered, I integrate them into your campaigns with proper A/B testing against existing winners.

You'll have direct access to me via phone and email for budget adjustments, service area changes, or strategic consultation. Management fee remains flat at $750/month regardless of how many campaigns we're running.

Static retargeting ads are delivered quarterly in batches of 3 (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4). These are designed to capture warm leads who watched your videos but didn't convert immediately. I handle all design, copywriting, technical setup of retargeting audiences, and campaign configuration.

Static ads run continuously alongside video campaigns and typically show to users 3-7 days after they've engaged with your video content.

The HousePro to Meta Conversions API integration happens during Month 1 and typically takes 1-2 weeks to complete. I'll need admin access to your HousePro account and Facebook Business Manager to configure the connection properly.

Once live, every time you mark a deal as "closed" in HousePro, that conversion data feeds back to Meta with the deal value. This enables true ROAS reporting and helps Meta's algorithm optimize campaigns for revenue instead of just lead volume.

This is a 12-month retainer agreement with monthly payments. You can cancel with 30 days written notice. Monthly retainer payments are non-refundable but cover work delivered cumulatively throughout the contract period.
